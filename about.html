A Few Words About Me

I am a senior at the University of Northern Colorado where I am getting my BFA in Musical Theatre as well as a minor in Digital Marketing. I am set to graduate in the spring of 2024 with Latin Honors!

Career Interests

My career interests are primarily focused on Musical Theatre. I want to pursue a career as an actor/singer. I aspire to perform in National Tours, International, and professional regional productions! Besides theatre, I also have interest in Digital Marketing! I am hoping to combine my interests and find a job in theatre/entertainment marketing! Check out my job resume!

Theatre Experience

Some of my favorite performance credits include Ella Understudy/Ensemble in "R&H Cinderella", Cinderella in "Into the Woods", Dawn in "Waitress", and Gertrude McFuzz in "Seussical". My passion for performing started at a young age and I have always loved doing theatre. Some of my special skills include British RP, Irish, and Southern Accents, some piano and guitar, puppeteering, splits, and comedic timing.

More About Me

I am a Colorado native, but I lived in New Zealand for two years from the ages of 10-12. I love travelling, cooking/baking, watching movies (specifically 90's Rom-Coms), and spending time with my friends!
